Strategy Pross and Cons: 

Rolling Update

Pros:
No downtime during updates
Gradual replacement of pods
Built-in rollback support

Cons:
Slower deployment process
Bad versions may partially affect users before rollback

Blue-Green Deployment

Pros:
Instant switch between versions
Fully tested environment before release

Cons:
Requires double resources (two environments)
More complex setup

Canary Deployment
Pros:
Low risk 
Real-world validation before full rollout
Fine-grained control over release

Cons:
Complex to configure
Requires monitoring and traffic splitting
