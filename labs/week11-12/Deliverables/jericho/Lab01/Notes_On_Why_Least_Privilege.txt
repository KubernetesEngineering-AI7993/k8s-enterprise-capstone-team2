The Role allows only get and list actions on Pods, which are read-only operations.

No permissions are granted for modifying resources (such as create, update, patch, or delete), preventing unintended or malicious changes.

Access is restricted to the dev namespace, ensuring the ServiceAccount cannot interact with resources in other namespaces.

The permissions are assigned to a dedicated ServiceAccount (readonly-sa), rather than broadly to users or groups, limiting the scope of access.

A Role is used instead of a ClusterRole, ensuring permissions are namespace-scoped and reducing the potential impact if the account is compromised.
